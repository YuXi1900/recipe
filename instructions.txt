Tomato chicken soup noodle
Step 1: Slice chicken breast into thin slices, add some salts, sit still for a while
Step 2: Dice tomato into small chunks
Step 3: Heat the pot, add cooking oil and dice tomato chunks, stir fry until tomato chunks break down
Step 4: Add chicken soup, bring to boiling
Step 5: Add noodle, stir and wait for boiling again
Step 6: Add sliced chicken breast, boil until chicken well cooked
